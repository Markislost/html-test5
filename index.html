The Best Movies According to John Mark

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spirited Away

is a beautifully crafted film that weaves together themes of self-discovery, resilience,
and the significance of connections with others. It remains a beloved and timeless
masterpiece that resonates with audiences of all ages around the world.

Ex Machina

is a thought-provoking science fiction film released in 2014, written and
directed by Alex Garland. The movie explores several complex themes and raises intriguing
questions about artificial intelligence, consciousness, human nature, and ethics.

Drive

The driver must navigate a treacherous underworld and use his skills behind the
wheel to protect the people he cares about and seek vengeance against those who threaten them.
"Drive" is praised for its stylish direction, intense action sequences, and Gosling's enigmatic performance.
